ABOUT US

Cool Beans Coffee Company was created in 2010 by an ambitious entrepreneur Kaylin Smith, because of his passion for the sweet nectar of coffee beans. The brand has now grown into multiple businesses all over Cape Town, and is still expanding, so that all can get their coffee fix no matter where they are.

All stores offer free Wi-Fi. We do the whole Facebook/Twitter thing because calling everyone when our new roasts are in is hurting our dialling finger – so if you want to be in the know, follow us.

We’re open to hosting your next shindig (big or small) and welcome you to chat to us.
